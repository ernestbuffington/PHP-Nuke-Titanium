CNB Your Account
The future of Phpnuke membership administration is here!

International Support
CNB Your Account is a collaboration between Phpnuke Brazil and Ravenphpscripts
and is the official continuation of the NSN Your Account module by Bob Marion of http://www.nukescript.net
Our International Support Forum can be found at http://ravenphpscripts.com/forum76.html

We would love to hear you ideas, suggestions, bugreports. Security, simplicity and functionality are our main focus.
If you want to help develop or want to be an active beta tester for our future release, please contact André V Escudero
